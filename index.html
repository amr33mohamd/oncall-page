OnCall is an application which provides information about doctors Oncall in hospitals and facilitates easy access to doctors in all sections of the hospital. Also, it presents details about the team on duty for each day. The goal is to reduce the time to search and reduce the cost of printing and distributing the papers. The application is available on the Apple and Android stores, The download is free. This work is personal and does not belong to any organization. Fees may be charged for uploading later.
